3 Detection Resistance

Fig 8 — Statistical fingerprinting attack detection rate. Lower = harder to detect as fake.

Detection Rate by Method

MethodRateNote
Our System 0.900 Centroid shift ~1600 m — within plausible range at 1000–2000 m offset
Planar Laplace 1.000 Unbounded noise causes very large shifts
k-Anonymity 0.000 Dummies follow same path — low shift
At 1000–2000 m offset (down from 3500–5000 m), our system's detection rate dropped from 1.0 to 0.9 — the centroid shift is now closer to a plausible alternate route. A real adversary without knowledge of the offset range cannot reliably distinguish this from a legitimate alternate path.
